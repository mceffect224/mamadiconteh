About Me

Building digital products that create impact

I am an accomplished IT Specialist, Full-Stack Engineer, and Technology Strategist with a passion for building innovative digital solutions. As Co-Founder of Lion Code Innovation, I lead the design and development of enterprise software, business automation platforms, educational management systems, e-commerce solutions, and digital transformation initiatives.
